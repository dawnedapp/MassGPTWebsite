GPT Mass Deleter Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

GPT Mass Deleter helps users bulk delete and archive ChatGPT conversations. This policy explains what data is processed and why.

Data We Process

  • Account credentials you submit (email and password) for authentication.
  • Authentication tokens used to keep you signed in.
  • Usage metadata for delete actions (count and timestamp) for rate limits and account features.
  • Subscription status information for Pro features (if billing is enabled).

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate accounts and secure access.
  • Enforce free-tier delete limits and admin/pro overrides.
  • Provide account features such as Pro access and support tooling.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is only shared with service providers required to operate the service, such as hosting, database, and billing providers.

Data Retention

Account and action-log data is retained as long as needed to operate the service and comply with legal obligations.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest.
